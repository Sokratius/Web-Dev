Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Ground beef 500g
Tomato sauce 400g
Onion 1, chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste

Step 1: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, cook until softened.

Step 2: Add ground beef to the pan and cook until browned.

Step 3: Pour in tomato sauce, add salt and pepper, and let simmer for 20 minutes.

Step 4: Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

Step 5: Serve the sauce over the spaghetti and enjoy!

Chicken Salad

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 2 pieces
Lettuce 1 head
Cucumber 1, sliced
Cherry tomatoes 10, halved
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste

Step 1: Grill or pan-fry the chicken breasts until fully cooked. Slice them into strips.

Step 2: In a bowl, combine chopped lettuce, sliced cucumber, and halved cherry tomatoes.

Step 3: Add the chicken strips to the salad and drizzle with olive oil.

Step 4: Season with salt and pepper, toss, and serve!
